Why Oak SEAMEI MUN?

Global Network, Local Impact

SEAMEI MUN is a launchpad for student diplomats across Southeast Asia to tackle real-world problems with bold, creative diplomacy.

Visionary Leadership

Led by a youth-driven executive team with international circuit experience, we create a meaningful and inclusive MUN experience.

Innovation in Debate

Expect crisis simulations, interdisciplinary committees, and never-before-seen agenda topics crafted to challenge conventional thinking.

Oakridge Quality

Hosted by one of the top international schools in the region, the conference promises academic excellence and organizational precision.

Awarded to the top-performing delegate in each committee, showcasing leadership, diplomacy, and innovation.

Outstanding Delegate

Recognizes consistent engagement, analytical skill, and respectful discourse throughout the sessions.

Commendable Delegate

Given to rising stars who bring unique perspectives and collaborative spirit to the debate.

Special Mention

Chosen by the Executive Board, this prize honors delegates with outstanding creativity and initiative.
