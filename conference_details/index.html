Frequently Asked Questions

Registration closes two weeks before the conference date. Early registration is recommended as spots are limited.

Please bring formal business attire, notebooks, pens, and any research materials. All other conference materials will be provided.

No prior experience is necessary. We welcome both novice and experienced delegates, with training sessions available for beginners.

Committee assignments are based on your preferences indicated during registration, experience level, and availability in each committee.

Prizes

Best Delegate

Awarded to the delegate who demonstrates exceptional diplomacy, leadership, and contribution to their committee's success.

Outstanding Delegate

Recognizes delegates who show remarkable debate skills, collaboration, and understanding of their country's position.

Commendable Delegate

Honors delegates who display good participation, research quality, and positive contribution to committee discussions.

Special Mentions

Acknowledges delegates who demonstrate unique insights, creativity, or significant improvement throughout the conference.
